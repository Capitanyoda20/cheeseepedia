Cheese's"] +++ Chuck E. Cheese's Photo Ride (1993) is a kiddie ride created in 1993 by AMUTEC its the first chuck e cheese photo ride and is the pizza time teathre version with the red derby

Gameplay

After inserting a token(play pass card) the ride begins with a back and forth rocking motion also insert a audio and also in the car they are 5 characters(munch make believe band members, chuck e , helen, jasper, munch and pasqually) buttons. At the end the camera takes a photo and the ride ends.

Design

It is a red car with lights and yellow wheels, windows with a seat and a statue of Chuck E with his PTT suit, with a camera that says "Take 5 with Chuck E", the car is on a blue base, inside there are 5 buttons. Also the chuck e statue have different variations some chucks have the cool/coach cap even the blue version some ride no have the chuck e statue, and now they are 2 chairs.
